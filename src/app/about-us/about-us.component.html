Think asset. Not Liability.

The greatest challenges in IT are operational, not technological. By its very definition legacy software serves a valuable purpose. It's an asset. An aging one, to be sure, but not one to treat with a cavalier “rip and replace” approach. That’s because, for most organizations, the greatest challenges are operational, not technological.
